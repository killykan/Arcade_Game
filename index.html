Princess in danger !!

Instructions to play

  • You play the little boy at the bottom left!
  • You must save the princess before the monster reaches her.
  • Your character can't swim, You must find a way to help him cross the river.
  • If you try to swim, your little boy will drown !
  • If your character gets hit by a rock, or by the monster, the game resets.
  • If the princess is eaten, the game will reset.
  • Beware of the monster, if you get too close to the princess, its speed will change.
  • Once you rescued the princess, head back to the shore, and reach the firecamp.
  • Enjoy !!
